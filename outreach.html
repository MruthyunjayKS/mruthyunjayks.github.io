Astrobiology Mission Control

Transform your classroom into a NASA command center. This fully interactive, web-based simulation assigns students to specialist teams (Navigation, Engineering, Science, Operations) to plan and execute a mission to Mars. They must collaborate to balance tight budgets, calculate orbital mechanics, and survive random mid-flight anomalies.
