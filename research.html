Survivability is just the baseline. Using comparative phylogenetics and metabolic modeling, I quantify the energy costs that constrain evolution. From the genome size of Archaea to the maintenance energy of Thermococcus kodakarensis, my work aims to define the 'thermodynamic evolvable space' of life.

Combining genomics, phylogenetics, and modeling to understand the limits of life
